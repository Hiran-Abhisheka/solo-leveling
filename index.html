Igris is a fictional character from the Korean web novel and manhwa series, Solo Leveling. Originally a powerful knight serving under the Demon King, he becomes one of Sung Jin-Woo’s loyal shadow soldiers after being defeated. Clad in crimson armor and wielding a greatsword, Igris is known for his unwavering loyalty, silent strength, and exceptional combat prowess, making him one of the most trusted and formidable warriors in Jin-Woo’s shadow army.
